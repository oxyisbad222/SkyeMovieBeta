You're one step away from experiencing the cutting-edge features of Skye Movie. Your feedback is crucial as we refine the app for its official launch.

This beta version provides early access to our innovative movie discovery tools. Please be aware that you might encounter occasional bugs or features that are still under development. We appreciate your patience and any reports you can provide.

To install the Skye Movie Beta on your iOS device:

  1. Tap the "Install iOS Beta" button below.
  2. Your device will ask: "This website is trying to download a configuration profile. Do you want to allow this?" Tap Allow.
  3. Open the Settings app on your device.
  4. Near the top, you should see a "Profile Downloaded" item. Tap it.
  5. Tap "Install" in the top-right corner, and follow the on-screen prompts (you may need to enter your device passcode).
  6. Once installed, the Skye Movie beta app icon will appear on your Home Screen.
